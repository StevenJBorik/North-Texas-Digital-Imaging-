CT-GE Lightspeed

Computerized Tomography (CT) uses low dose X-rays at different angles and the computer generates a 3D image. CT or “CAT scans” are preferred to evaluate bone/ joint conditions and utilized for a broad area of study, such as a generalized view of vessels, organs, or body region. Our GE Lightspeed produces high resolution and faster scans.

{% set image_src = './src/images/mri.jpg' %} {% set image_config = { 'class': 'tech-img-src', 'loading': 'lazy' } %} {% image image_src, image_config %}

MRI - GE Signa Echospeed Excite 1.5T

MRI uses Magnetic Resonance to produce 3D images. Clinicians prefer MRI for more detailed images of a focal targeted tissue. The Excite Accelerator technology reduces exam time without sacrificing spatial resolution. The advantage: Shorter patient exam time and eliminates motion artifacts. Comfort; the wide aperture & short bore design eliminates claustrophobia.

{% set image_src = './src/images/hologic.jpg' %} {% set image_config = { 'class': 'tech-img-src', 'loading': 'lazy' } %} {% image image_src, image_config %}

Hologic QDR 4500 Bone Densitometer

Hologic QDR-4500c has the combination of the superiority of fan-beam technology with a unique, value-added package of clinical, patient reporting, and support software. Hologic QDR-4500c uses a user-friendly operating system that provides step-by-step instructions through the examination process with accurate results at the push of a button. The unit is nearly five times faster than the time it takes to produce similar, clinically-meaningful scans than the fastest pencil-beam system. This fast scanning method allows you to contribute to your potential for practice growth and can accommodate more patients in your busy schedule.

X-Ray

X-ray is convenient to capture bone and regions such as the chest for study. The digital images can be viewed online or on a CD rather than traditional films.

Ultrasound - Sequoia Acuson 512

The Aspen is the result of selected Acuson Siemens Sequoia technologies and other Acuson technologies combined to form a whole new ultrasound imaging platform. The Acuson Aspen has dual linear connection capabilities and Native Tissue Harmonic Imaging (NTHI). With applications in vascular, obstetrical, cardiac, and small parts.
