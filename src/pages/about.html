About Us

North Texas Digital Imaging has been providing a range of healthcare services to residents of Forney and the surrounding areas since the fall of 2007. NTDI was known as Forney Diagnostic Imaging until movingvto Mesquite in 2010. We are excited to extend our services to more physicians and patients. Our knowledgeable team of caring professionals has been dedicated to providing state of the art radiological services to their patients. We take pride in our staff of Board Certified radiologists.

All of our technical staff is nationally registered in the modality they perform. Some hold advanced registries in multiple areas and have undergone extensive training on our equipment and imaging procedures.
